# Health Benchmarks and Guidelines

These standard health benchmarks are used for comparing user data in `apple-health-analyst` reports.

## Activity Benchmarks

- **Step Count:** 10,000 steps per day is a common goal for active adults.
- **Active Energy:** Calorie goals vary based on age, gender, and activity level (common ranges: 300-600 kcal for daily active energy).
- **Exercise Time:** 30 minutes of moderate-intensity activity per day is recommended for heart health.
- **Stand Hours:** A goal of 12 hours with at least one minute of movement each hour is standard for reducing sedentary time.

## Vital Benchmarks

- **Resting Heart Rate (RHR):** A typical RHR for adults ranges from 60 to 100 beats per minute (BPM). Athletes may have lower RHRs (40-60 BPM).
- **Sleep Duration:** 7-9 hours of sleep per night is recommended for most adults.
- **Respiratory Rate:** A normal resting respiratory rate for an adult is 12 to 20 breaths per minute.

## Analysis Patterns

- **Week-over-Week (WoW):** Compare current week totals/averages with the previous week.
- **Monthly Trends:** Identify long-term changes in weight, RHR, or overall activity.
- **Goal Completion:** Calculate the percentage of days the user met their calorie, exercise, or stand goals.
